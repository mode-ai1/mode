Evidence-Backed Pedagogy

Proven Transfer (g ≈ 0.49)

MØDE's central premise—code as a medium for learning maths—has strong meta-analytic support. Programming produces a moderate overall transfer to mathematical skill, strongest when the target skill is close to the code.

📈

Socratic Escalation

A Socratic AI tutor beats group instruction (g ≈ 0.42), but novices need scaffolding. MØDE escalates support: Question → Hint → Partial Example → Direct Explanation to prevent working-memory overload.

🧩

The AI / Agent Stack

4 Real Layers

MØDE is built on real, interoperable, and documented technology. It is not just "an AI" — it is a precise orchestration of four layers.

Orchestrator
Hermes Agent: Runtime with persistent cross-session memory + autonomous skill loop.
Reasoning Brain
NVIDIA Nemotron 3 Ultra: Routed by difficulty, with reasoning-budget control.
Safety Cage
NVIDIA NemoClaw: OpenShell sandbox, declarative egress policy, providing the mechanism for MØDE-authored safety policies.
Wallet
Stripe Agent Toolkit: Scoped, spend-capped, delegated agent identities to pay for services.

Bounded Business Autonomy

Humans on the Loop

Structurally Low Marginal Cost

Rather than claiming "$0 overhead", MØDE leverages cost levers like serverless hibernation, model right-sizing, and reasoning-budget caps to achieve structurally low marginal cost and minimal fixed overhead.

Bounded Operations

Learning from Anthropic's Project Vend, MØDE utilizes bounded autonomy: short, checkpointed, recoverable steps run within hard limits (scoped Stripe keys, spend caps, NemoClaw egress rules), with humans owning judgement calls.

Child Safety & Privacy

The Gating Constraint

A product for 5–15-year-olds triggers the strictest tier of privacy law in every major market. NemoClaw provides the mechanism, but MØDE authors and tests the child-safety policies.

🇺🇸

COPPA (US)

Verifiable parental consent, separate consent for third-party disclosure, and data-minimisation.

🇬🇧

Children's Code (UK)

High privacy by default, profiling off, and no nudge techniques (careful gamification).

🇪🇺

GDPR & POPIA

Strict design tension resolved: store pedagogical state (mastery, ZPD position) rather than raw session transcripts.
